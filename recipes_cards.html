PASTITSIO

Ingredients

For the ground meat

  • 750 g ground beef
  • 2 onions
  • 1 clove(s) of garlic
  • 4 tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon(s) granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon(s) salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon(s) black pepper
  • 2 tablespoon(s) tomato paste
  • 400 g canned tomatoes
  • 1 stick cinnamon
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4-5 bachar balls
  • 1/2 bunch parsley, finely chopped

For the bechamel sauce

  • 100gr butter
  • 100gr all-purpose-flour
  • 1lt whole milk
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon(s) nutmeg
  • 100g pecorino cheese, grated
  • 2 medium eggs

For the pasta

  • 400gr bucatini pasta
  • 30ml olive oil
  • 100gr hard salted cheese, grated

To assemble

  • 150gr gouda cheese, grated

Execution Method

For the ground meat

  • Place a pan over high heat.
  • Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil, the finely chopped onions and 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • Sauté for 3-4 minutes, until the onions caramelize nicely.
  • Add the garlic and continue to sauté.
  • Add the tomato paste and sauté for at least 2 minutes. It is important to sauté the tomato paste to give it a more intense flavor.
  • Add the ground meat. Break it up with a wooden spoon and brown for 4-5 minutes. At this point you may need to add another 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
  • Add the canned tomatoes, cinnamon, bacher and bay leaves.
  • Turn the heat down to low and simmer for 10-20 minutes, until the sauce reduces and the ground meat is cooked. You may need to add 1/2cup of water.
  • When ready, remove from heat. Discard the bay leaves, bachar, cinnamon stick and set aside until needed.

For the bechamel sauce

  • Heat the butter in a pot over low heat. As soon as it melts (do not let it burn), set the pot aside and add the flour. Beat with a hand whisk and sauté the flour for a few minutes.
  • Add the 1/3 of the cold milk, whisking continuously so that no lumps form in the mixture.
  • whisk the eggs and add them in your mixture. In that stage is cold. Mix well and then put again on the stove over low-medium heat.
  • Add the rest of the milk, in small batches and whisking continuously.
  • When start thicknener add the salt, nutmeg, and the grated cheese.
  • When ready, remove from heat and set aside until needed.

For the bechamel sauce

  • Add the pasta to a pot full of salted water. Boil 1-2 minutes less than the instructions on the box. They need to be al dente because they will cook further in the oven, so do not overcook.
  • When the pasta is ready, drain, add the olive oil and bring back to the stove. Let it for 1-2 minutes, mix
  • Transfer the pasta in a big pyrex, add the cheese and mix.

To assemble

  • Preheat oven to 180* C Fan.
  • Bring back the baking pan containing the pasta mixture.
  • To the ground meat, add a ladleful of the béchamel sauce. Mix until completely incorporated. The béchamel sauce will make the ground meat mixture hold together better when serving.
  • Spread the ground meat mixture over the pasta in the baking pan and mix
  • Pour the remaining béchamel sauce over the ground meat.
  • Last, sprinkle the gouda cheese and left overs from the pecorino or any other salted hard cheese over the top.
  • Bake for 40 minutes or until the béchamel turns golden brown.
